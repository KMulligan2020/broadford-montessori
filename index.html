About us

A little school with a big heart

Broadford Montessori is a small, nurturing preschool where children are free to explore, create and grow at their own pace.

We believe the early years are about so much more than getting ready for school. They are about building confidence, curiosity, and a love of learning that lasts a lifetime. Our sessions are intentionally small so that every child is truly known and every family feels at home.

We follow the Aistear framework, Ireland's early childhood curriculum, and are fully registered with Tusla.
